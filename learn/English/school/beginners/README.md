## Adverbs of frequency

Adverbs of frequency

* 100% Always
* 90% Usually
* 80% Frequently
* 70% Often
* 30% Occasionally
* 10% Seldom
* 5% Rarely
* 0% Never

<div align="center">
  <img src="img/9.png">
</div>

**Rules for adverbs of frequency:**
Noun + frequency + action
Example: I never drink coffe.

**There is one exception to this rule** --> Sometimes
Example: Sometimes I go to the beach.
